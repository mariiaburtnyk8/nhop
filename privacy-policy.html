Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

National House of Pancakes ("NHOP", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you visit our website or make a reservation online.

1. Information We Collect

When you make a reservation through our website, we collect the following information:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Reservation date, time, and number of guests
  • Any special notes or requests you choose to include

We do not collect payment information, social security numbers, or any other sensitive financial or identity data through this website.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Confirm and manage your reservation
  • Send you a booking confirmation email
  • Contact you if there are any changes or issues with your reservation
  • Improve our service and guest experience

We do not use your information for marketing purposes without your explicit consent, and we do not sell or rent your data to third parties.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your reservation data is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database platform with industry-standard encryption. Data is stored on servers in the United States.

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of internet transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Email Communications

By submitting a reservation, you may receive transactional emails related to your booking — such as a confirmation email when your reservation is confirmed. These are not marketing emails and are sent only in connection with your reservation.

Transactional emails are sent via Brevo (formerly Sendinblue), a third-party email service provider. We share only the information necessary to deliver those emails.

5. Cookies

Our website does not currently use tracking cookies or third-party analytics tools. We may use essential cookies necessary for the site to function properly.

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate this website:

  • Supabase — database and backend infrastructure
  • Brevo — transactional email delivery
  • Vercel — website hosting
  • Cloudinary — image hosting

Each of these providers has their own privacy policy and data practices. We encourage you to review them if you have concerns.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time (where applicable)

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us directly using the details below.

8. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted information through our site, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
