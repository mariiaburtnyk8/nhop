Fan Favorites

Fan favorites, here to stay. Stack 'em high, flip 'em fresh, and pour a generous puddle of maple — your booth's already warm.

Wildberry Stack

Wild blueberries, raspberries, lemon-zest mascarpone, and a slow-pour citrus curd. Bright, tart, stacked tall.

S'mores Soirée

Marshmallow fluff, dark-chocolate ganache, graham-cracker crumble. Campfire energy — best eaten while it's still steaming.

Chocolate & Reese's

Cocoa-batter pancakes, melted Reese's cups, dark-fudge ribbon, salty peanut crunch. Gone five minutes after arrival.

Nutella Banana Blast

Nutella ribbons, banana coins, fresh strawberries, icing sugar. Childhood breakfast — upgraded.

Chicken & Waffles

Buttermilk chicken tenders on a Belgian waffle, scallion, chili honey. Sweet, salty, crunchy — built for a lazy brunch.

Shrimp & Grits

Stone-ground grits, blackened gulf shrimp, cajun butter, scallion, lemon. Low-country comfort — exactly as it should be.
